Satoru Gojo

Satoru Gojo is a protagonist character from the manga/anime called Jujutsu Kaisen. Satoru is known as the "Strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer" and is depicted as a playful character towards his students and friends; however, he blatantly disrespects sorcerer executives like Principal Gakuganji.

Admirable Qualities of the Character:

  • As seen in the animated GIF - Satoru Gojo is a carefree, extraverted guy who likes to goof around with his friends and students.
  • Satoru's abilities are extremely overpowered and help justify his title as the "Strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer."
  • According to Fandom, Satoru wants to "reform the jujutsu world from the bottom-up through education and foster a new generation of sorcerers that he hopes will one day become his equals."
